In the Digital Age, universities require modern, digital solutions

Challenge

There are multiple ERP systems available online but what we forget is that every university has itâ€™s own internal system of doing things and with that, the students also face a distinct set of problems.






Solution

An app that would go beyond keeping students on time with their submissions.
A university-centric platform that would create a hassle-free life for the students of World University of Design, Sonipat, Haryana by catering to their specific needs.

User Research

I started user research to analyze and empathize with the target group to gain more insight with regard to the challenges faced by them.

1. Survey

I conducted an online survey of 50 students of World University of Design whose age is between 19-22.


Through this survey, I realized that the majority of the students face common problems.




2. Interview

To understand the viewpoint of the students better, I conducted 10 in-person interviews which provided me with grave insight into the students minds and empathize with these problems better.

Key Findings

→

95% of students have faced an issue to find transport and carpool to travel anywhere outside the campus with a minimum of 20 minutes wait time.

→

Day Scholars are unable to produce cash for mess coupons every day & there is excess rush for coupons.

→

A major issue faced by the students is getting the gatepass signed by the faculty, dean and the warden as one or the other is not available.

→

Many students wish to be able to attend and study other departments subjects but the overlap of timetable and time constraints does not allow for them to do that

→

Students miss out on events happening around the campus.

→

Students find it difficult to collaborate and share ideas with other departments

And many more...

Brainstorming

After a session of brainstorming, I identified the following features:

1. Alumni Network

This Network space would help students connect to the alumniâ€™s and learn more about the industry.

2. Clubs

This will encourage students to stay informed about the club and their assignments and competitions in the same domain.

3. Digital Gatepass

This Digital System will allow students to keep a track of their gatepasses and it will reduce the wastage of paper.

4. Hostel

It is for students using the hostel facility to know about the important updates, their attendance, etc.

5. Placements

This section will inform the students about the placements experiences of the students to help the juniors prepare better.

6. To-Do List

It will help students make a note of all the important, upcoming tasks and meet the deadline

7. Online Courses

It will help students expand their horizon of knowledge without skipping classes.

8. Transport

Through this section, students can easily find other students who wish to carpool and also contact student trusted and recommended drivers.

9. Upcoming Events

This feature will inform the students about all the upcoming events in the college.

10. Coursework

This section includes all the assignments, course breakdown, attendance, grades, etc.

11. Meal Coupons

Maintaining cash is difficult as the college is in the outskirts of the city. It will also allow students who eat daily in the mess to buy coupons in bulk and skip long lines.

12. Library

All books in the library will be listed here along with their availability. Moreover, the e-books will be ready for download with one click

Crazy 8s for the Homepage

After this exercise I finalized 3 homepage designs and I was confused about which one to add. I couldnâ€™t think of a better idea than TEST the homepage on the users and make the decision based on their feedback!

To test this I created a sample upcoming events section and sidebar menu and combined all of them to create a simple prototype for the users.

I tested the homepages on 10 distinct users giving them a simple task of navigating to the upcoming events section. After analyzing their response, I asked them questions about the homepage separately for each of the designs. These question included, what they thought was lacking, how friendly was the interface, was it easy to navigate to the upcoming events section, which design fulfilled their needs, etc.
