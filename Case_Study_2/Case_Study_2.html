Problem
Identification

How might we help refugees and migrants seek help legally, medically and support in resettlement from the comfort of their phones so that they can receive immediate help and attention required.

There were 20.7 million refugees recorded around the world
at the end of 2020, but less than one per cent of refugees
are resettled each year.

Let's
Understand

Why are there Refugees in the first place?

After my research I came up with a list of top reasons which forces people to displace

1. Persecution

The most common reason people become refugees is persecution — which can take on many forms: religious, national, social, racial, or political.

2. War

Most of history’s refugees have been the direct or indirect product of war. Currently, a large group of refugees are fleeing civil conflict in Ukraine.

3. Hunger

It’s estimated that 20 million people from North African and Middle Eastern countries are facing extreme drought, and many of these individuals are becoming refugees, forced from their homelands in search of stable food sources.

5. Violation of Human Rights

6. Economic Crisis

Importance/ Urgency of the matter

To understand the topic better it is important to know the importance of the topic and its' relevance in today's time

- In light of the ongoing humanitarian crisis in Ukraine, millions of refugees from Ukraine have crossed borders into neighboring countries, and many more have been forced to relocate inside the country. They are in urgent need of protection and support.

- These refugees have to resettle their lives in a completely unfamiliar place while tackling with issues like acquiring legal status, language barrier, trauma due to the war, social adaptation, health and many more. We hope to help these refugees restart their lives in an easier manner.

For Refugees, Smartphones are their lifeline.

- According to a UNHCR Report, there is a misconception that refugees live in low network connectivity areas but the truth is that more than 80 percent of the refugees are hosted by developing countries which are connected with at least a 2G network.

- For the refugees, their phones provide them hope of connecting with their family left behind and surviving in an alien country.

Design Process

User Story

User Story is a great method to empathize with the target audience better and understand their situation

Larissa and her family were living peaceful lives before the war began. With her two daughters Angelina and Alina was forced to leave her peaceful neighbourhood. They fled to Poland for safety.

1. Personal Level

Larissa has received enormous support as a refugee from people all over the world but she has also faced discrimination. The language barrier makes it harder for her to communicate. She is in dire need of medical help for her sick child and has noone to turn to. She also needs to get her legal status in Poland but does not have time or money to consult a lawyer

2. System Level

On a system level, the NGOs and the organisations are doing the best they can but there is a shortage of volunteers on site. The time taken to fix an in-person appointment for free legal and medical consultations is time consuming and not viable.

3. Global Level

Larissa represents many refugees out there. Globally, they faces difficulties adapting socially which in-turn restricts their knowledge about the local facilities, organisations and transportations. Global shortages of resources which has a profound impact on the resettlement of these refugees. We are faced with the stark reality of these refugees. They are confronted with challenges everyday struggling after fleeing their homelands.

Brainstorming

Final Solution and Innovation

By connecting refugees to lawyers, medical professionals, local communities and organizations we aim to help them make their process of resettlement easier.

How is it innovative?

- We provide online consultation with best professionals in their respective fields from the comfort of their location. The app is not restricted to legal and medical aid but also, connects refugees to organizations targeting specific needs like helping children receive education. Moreover, it gives access to offline maps locating nearby organizations and information about how to navigate through the city.


-The application allows the users and trusted volunteers from different services to manage, update and track the documents while also communicating with the refugees making the whole process paperless and painless.


-Our aim was to integrate all the features in one app and match them with the right professionals to provide them with easy, online consultation.

- Connectx app shows refugees and migrants their location and connects them to services to fulfill their needs. It also helps you save a digital copy of all your important documents

- After the user selects their need, they will be connected with the right professional and then, they will be redirected to a screen to schedule an online appointment with them.

Impact on the Society

- By connecting refugees to receive aid through online consultation, our solution will create a safe and secure network which will be convenient for both the parties and help give equal priority to every refugee and decrease the cost.

- Our aim is to ease the process of resettlement and help refugees in as many areas as possible within one app.

- Refugees are innocent people who are forced to flee their country because of war or violence. They didn’t choose to uproot their lives. Adjusting in an unknown territory with an entirely different culture is hard in itself.

- Our app will connect them to resources they require to adapt to the new world and cater to their legal and medical needs.

- Along with which, it aims to make them independent by helping navigate through the city. This in itself will help them break the boundaries and understand the new way of life.

Sustainability

- Our app is not only viable in refugee crisis but also during economic crisis which leads to an increase in forceful migration.

- According to Reuters report, in 2022 13 countries are said to be at risk of an economic crisis. We are no strangers to the Venezualen crisis and till date there have been over 6 million refugees and migrants through that itself.

- With an increase in economic crisis and war worldwide, our app will help in bringing equal medical and legal facility to every refugee.

- This is made possible through online consultation and also, help them reach out to the local organizations and make their process of resettlement easier.

- Connectx is going to sustain till there are refugees and migrants all over the world and considering the year by year increase in crisis in different parts of the world, it is not going to end anytime soon. This makes it a long lasting solution.

Practicality & Business Viability

- Wars produce refugees. People will continue to move to improve their quality of life – not only because of extreme poverty, but because they are connected to global culture and global networks of communication. Climate change has the potential to create far greater displacement than we have seen in recent years; as with refugees from war, it is likely to be poorer countries who feel the greatest impact. This is a crisis that cannot be solved in the near future and that is why this is a timely and a long term solution which can be tweaked easily.

- The simple and easy to understand interface the app making it easy for elder, younger or less tech-savvy to easily understand and use.
